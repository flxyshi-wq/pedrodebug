FAQ

Common questions

Is Pedro Debug detected on DonutSMP?
Pedro Debug is currently undetected when used alongside OpSec Mod. Modules are patched within 24 hours of DonutSMP server updates, but no client can guarantee permanent detection avoidance.
What does the deepslate bypass do?
It renders terrain normally obstructed by DonutSMP's deepslate protection layer, allowing navigation through restricted areas without triggering server-side flags.
Does Pedro Debug work with my launcher?
Yes. Pedro Debug runs on Fabric, which is supported by Modrinth App, Prism Launcher, Feather Launcher, Lunar Client, and most other major launchers.
Do I really need OpSec Mod?
Yes — it's mandatory. OpSec Mod protects your account from DonutSMP's anti-cheat. Running Pedro Debug without it puts your account at risk.
How does the spawner notifier work?
It scans the loaded world for mob spawners and marks each one with a vertical beam visible through terrain, updating in real time as you move.
